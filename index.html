About Me

Hi! My name is James Elms. I am a dedicated and skilled software developer with a passion for creating impactful solutions. I am based in Hampshire, United Kingdom.


I have experience particularly with .NET technologies. My proficiency includes C#, ASP.NET, EF Core, Razor Pages, Git/GitHub, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Throughout my projects, I have gained valuable experience that complements my previous work in other industries, and I am eager to continue my career in .NET development.


I enjoy using C# and .NET to build solutions, but I also have an interest in Swift for development on Apple products, working with Unreal Engine on personal projects, and experimenting with other programming languages or frameworks that peak my interest like Vue, React, Angular, CodeIgniter, Python or C++. I like to explore other avenues, whilst honing my existing .NET skills, for development to build upon my understanding of the industry and personal skillset.


I hold several CCNA networking certificates that I had the opportunity to study whilst at university. This sparked an interest in networking and I have recently been experimenting with home servers and networking devices as a hobby. This has provided me the opportunity to experiment with deploying services like version control or web servers, and explore the security risks and considerations involved in networking and delivering services.
