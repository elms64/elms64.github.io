About Me

I am a dedicated and skilled software developer with a passion for creating impactful solutions. One of my notable projects was designing, creating, and deploying a fully functional inventory management and booking system for my university's media department. This application has been highly successful, enabling students to view and book media equipment while allowing teachers to efficiently manage loans and inventory. The system integrates authentication with the existing Microsoft Entra tenant using the Identity Framework and Graph API and is deployed on Azure App Service.


I have experience in software development, particularly with .NET technologies. My proficiency includes C#, ASP.NET, EF Core, Razor Pages, Git/GitHub, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Throughout my projects, I have gained valuable experience that complements my previous work in other industries, and I am eager to continue my career in .NET development.
